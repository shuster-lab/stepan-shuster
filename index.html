frequently asked
FAQ
[01] What services do you offer? SERVICES +

UX/UI design and web design — covering the full cycle from user research and wireframes to interactive prototypes and final handoff. I work on both product interfaces and marketing websites.

[02] What tools do you work with? TOOLS +

Figma for UX/UI design, wireframing and prototyping. Framer for web design and development. I hand off clean, well-organised files that are easy for developers to work with.

[03] What is your typical timeline? TIMELINE +

A landing page or simple website takes around 2–3 weeks. More complex UX projects range from 4–8 weeks depending on scope and feedback cycles. Timelines are always agreed upfront.

[04] Do you work with international clients? CLIENTS +

Yes — I'm based in Prague and have worked on projects for clients across the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Russia, and Georgia. I communicate fluently in English, Czech, and Russian.

[05] Are you available for full-time roles? AVAILABILITY +

Currently exploring both freelance projects and new full-time opportunities. If you have something interesting in mind — let's talk.
